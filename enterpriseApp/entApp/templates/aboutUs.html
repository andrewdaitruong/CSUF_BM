About Us

Fullerton Black Market is a site for helping members of the California State University of Fullerton (CSUF), both students and faculty save money on school necessities. We wanted to help those who are having financial difficulty and let them save more of their own money. We offer discounts to students and faculties so long as they have a valid CSUF email.

Project Idea: The idea of the project stemmed from having to make an ecommerce website and what we thought about was how to help students in need
